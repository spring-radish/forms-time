all form forms time / falls on the body

a divinatory are.na mood tracker

dedicated to kari edwards <3

before you are 366 spaces. enter a public are.na url (block or user) to see the blocks fall into their days, 100 at a time. all years are layered on top of each other, to surface seasonal cycles between january and december. the tool supports diary channels by preferring dates that appear in a block’s title, followed by date connected and date created.
